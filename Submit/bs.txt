Controls:
Use the touchpad or mouse for interactions.

Moving a cube - select a cube by pressing on it, then drag it to move.
Expanding a cube - select a cube by pressing on it, then press the expand button. You can then hover over any of its side, and drag to resize the cube. Once you are done, press the expand button once again.
Deleting a cube - select a cube by pressing on it, then press and hold the red trash icon.
